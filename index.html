Civil Engineering License Syllabus

You can go through Civil Engineering License exam syllabus provided by Nepal Engineering Council. You can also go through the practice MCQ's chapterwise and topicwise.
We have also provided the downloading option for the syllabus where you can download the syllabus and go through it.
There are 10 chapters to study for Civil Engineering License Council Exam. There also sub-topics in every chapter. They are listed as:

  1. Basic Civil Engineering
  2. Soil Mechanics and Foundation Engineering
  3. Basic Water Resources Engineering
  4. Structural Mechanics
  5. Design of Structures
  6. Water Supply, Sanitation and Environment
  7. Irrigation and Drainage
  8. Hydropower
  9. Transportation
  10. Project Planning, Design and Implementation
